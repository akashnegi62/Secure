FAQ

Questions worth asking before you sign

How long does a typical installation take?

A single-site CCTV or access control rollout usually takes 3–7 working days after the design is approved. Multi-site or integrated deployments are scheduled site by site to avoid disrupting operations.

Do you work with our existing equipment?

In most cases, yes. Our technicians audit what's on-site during the survey and reuse compatible cabling, panels, and cameras wherever it doesn't compromise coverage or reliability.

Are your guards licensed and insured?

Every guard is licensed, background-verified, and covered under our liability insurance before being deployed to a site.

What happens if a system goes down at 2am?

Full Site and Enterprise plans include 24/7 monitored response — our operations desk is notified automatically and dispatches a technician or guard depending on the fault.
